Our Story

Welcome to Tailored Pieces Ug. We are a leader in both traditional African outfits and urban fashion styles in Makindye Luwafu. We offer both rental and custom outfits for all ages, for both men and women. You’ll find dresses and accessories full of whimsy, femininity, and charm — perfect for flower girls, photography, and much more.

On February 20th, 2019, Tailored Pieces Ug was founded.

Our 3-year journey has been quite eventful but fulfilling, growing our portfolio from fashion and entertainment to the hospitality business.

From the beginning, Tailored Pieces Ug set out to create a world-class product and service tailored for the modern, fashion-conscious consumer.

Our approach puts class and exquisite taste at the forefront of our products, giving our clientele a once-in-a-lifetime, unforgettable experience.

We thrive on creativity and flexibility, allowing us to cater to each client's unique needs.

Why Choose Tailored Pieces Ug?

At Tailored Pieces Ug, we’re more than just a fashion brand — we’re a lifestyle. Whether you're looking for an elegant traditional African outfit, a bold urban look, or the perfect accessory for a special occasion, we’re committed to delivering high-quality, custom pieces that match your unique taste.

Our skilled designers and seamstresses ensure that every item reflects your personality and style, so you always feel confident and beautiful.

Our Vision

At Tailored Pieces Ug, we envision a world where fashion is a true expression of individuality. We celebrate diversity through clothing and offer designs that help every person look and feel their best.

Our goal is to become the top fashion choice for enthusiasts in Uganda and beyond — blending culture, creativity, and class in every garment we make.
